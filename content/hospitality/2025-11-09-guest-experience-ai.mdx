## Map the signature moments

Every Atlas stay is plotted around three signature moments: arrival, transition, and closure. We script the emotion we want each moment to create, then ask: where can AI amplify the teamâ€™s intuition?

## Give AI the backstage, not the stage

We do not let AI speak first. Instead, an orchestrator agent listens to guest signals, drafts suggested replies, and alerts the human host when an inflection point appears.

<PullQuote>
Hospitality should feel improvisational to the guest and meticulously rehearsed behind the scenes.
</PullQuote>

## Measure the rituals that matter

Rather than NPS, we track ritual completion: did the arrival tea ceremony happen? Did the personalized playlist trigger during sunset? AI alerts us when rituals slip so a human can course correct.
